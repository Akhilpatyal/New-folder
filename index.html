Introducing Brigade Yelahanka – Exquisite 3/4 BHK Apartments in Yelahanka, North Bengaluru.

Brigade Yelahanka is a premium high-rise residential enclave that sets a new benchmark for luxury living in North Bangalore. Spread over an expansive 14 acres, this meticulously planned township is designed to harmonize modern lifestyles with nature. With 80% of its area devoted to lush greenery and recreational spaces, it offers residents a serene escape amidst the hustle and bustle of urban life. Developed by the esteemed Brigade Group, this project is a testament to their commitment to excellence and innovation in real estate.

Brigade Yelahanka feels much like the chosen land, with exquisite residences and a classic rooftop retreat for intimate gatherings. Light and air play a playground here during the day, and evenings bring along musical notes of starry nights.
